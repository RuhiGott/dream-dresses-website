Who We Are

We are a 501(c)3 nonprofit that provides a platform for you to donate your traditional Indian clothing to underprivileged kids in India.

Ship Dresses

We distribute dresses to our three partner organizations: Catalysts for Social Action, Aarna Foundation, and Goonj. Our aim is to specifically donate to families under the poverty line who cannot afford these beautiful, traditional dresses.
